Race Timing

This event will be chip-timed by Race Timing Unlimited, which means all times will be accurate, and result processing almost instantaneous. Please take the time to read more about race day details regarding the timing system at www.racetimingunlimited

Race Photos

Photos from prior years races can be found on the TriDuo's website. Once again this year they will be photographing the runners, and will be available for purchase after the race.

Thank You

The Short Pump Race thanks its sponsors, volunteers and schools who participated in this event. In 2010, we had 595 registered runners, representing over a thirty percent increase in participation compared to 2009. This youth race continues to be a great success!
