Sponsorship Opportunities

We welcome any business to join our elite group of sponsors who help make the Short Pump Mile Race and Express Run a special event, and also an affordable one for many families. Not only do you help children of the region learn about how much fun running can be, but you will also be helping out area youth with your donation.

We have two levels of sponsorship available at this time. The first is 'Heat Sponsorship' as you can see from not only the first section of our website, but also on the heat list, you will get maximum exposure for your business.

The next level is 'Associate Sponsorship' - this is a smaller donation, but will also give you some web visibility and other options to promote your business.

For more information regarding sponsorship - please contact us!
